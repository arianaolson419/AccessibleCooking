Chocolate Chip Cookies

About

Description

A classic! The most delicious chocolate chip cookies you will ever have.

Difficulty

Intermediate

Time

45 minutes

Servings

4 dozen 3 inch cookies

Equipment

  • Oven Ways to navigate your oven
  • Baking Sheet
  • Whisk or Mixer

Ingredients

  • 1 cup butter, softened
  • 1 cup white sugar
  • 1 cup packed brown sugar
  • 2 eggs
  • 2 teaspoons vanilla extract
  • 3 cups all purpose flour
  • 1 teaspoon baking soda
  • 2 teaspoons hot water
  • One half teaspoon salt
  • 2 cups semisweet chocolate chips

Ingredients

  1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees Fahrenheit (175 degrees Celsius)
  2. Cream together butter, white sugar, and brown sugar until smooth. How to cream butter and sugar
  3. Beat in eggs one at a time, then stir in vanilla.
  4. Dissolve baking soda in hot water. Add to batter along with salt.
  5. Stir in flour.
  6. Stir in chocolate chips.
  7. Drop by large spoonfuls onto ungreased pans.
  8. Bake for about 10 minutes in the preheated oven, or until the edges are firm but the center is soft.
